About Blanchard Designs

We believe in the power of minimalism and the beauty of simplicity

Our Philosophy

At Blanchard Designs, we create spaces that breathe. Our approach combines minimal aesthetics with functional beauty, ensuring every element serves a purpose while contributing to the overall harmony of your environment.


With over a decade of experience, we've refined our process to deliver designs that stand the test of time, focusing on quality materials, thoughtful layouts, and the perfect balance between form and function.
